HiTech (“we,” “our,” or “us”) is developed by ByteLab, based in Germany. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the HiTech AR mirror experience.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or would like to contact us regarding privacy-related matters, you can reach us at:

ByteLab
Website: byte-lab.tech
Email: info@byte-lab.tech

1. About HiTech

HiTech is a smart mirror AR experience created for entertainment and showcasing different types of augmented reality experiences. It allows users to view AR effects in real time through the mirror using camera-based tracking features such as face tracking, body tracking, and hand tracking.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or sell personal data through HiTech.

More specifically:

  • We do not require users to create an account.
  • We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or other contact details.
  • We do not collect payment information.
  • We do not collect analytics data, advertising identifiers, or crash reports.
  • We do not store camera images, videos, face scans, body scans, hand scans, or biometric templates.
  • We do not store user-generated content.

3. Camera and AR Processing

HiTech uses the device camera in real time to power augmented reality experiences.

This processing may include:

  • face tracking
  • body tracking
  • hand tracking

This processing happens on-device and in real time for the purpose of displaying AR effects only.

  • We do not use this functionality to identify users personally.
  • We do not create or store biometric templates, facial recognition data, or similar records.
  • We do not save camera footage or tracking data.

4. Photos and Recordings

HiTech does not automatically take or store photos, videos, or recordings.

If a user chooses to record or photograph the mirror experience using their own phone or another external device, that recording is made outside of HiTech and is governed by the policies and settings of that device or platform, not by us.

If in the future HiTech includes an optional feature that allows a user to request a direct capture, save, or export function, this Privacy Policy may be updated accordingly.

5. How We Use Information

Because we do not collect or store personal data through HiTech, we do not use personal information for purposes such as:

  • account management
  • advertising
  • analytics
  • profiling
  • marketing
  • data resale
  • user tracking

The only technical processing performed is the temporary, real-time on-device processing needed to render AR effects.

6. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information.

We do not share personal data with advertisers, marketing platforms, or business partners because we do not collect such data through HiTech.

HiTech uses a Camera Kit provider to enable AR functionality. To the extent that camera-based AR technology is used, it is used solely to power the live experience as described above.

7. Data Retention

We do not retain personal data from users of HiTech because no personal data is collected or stored through the experience.

8. Children’s Privacy

HiTech is intended for a general audience and is not specifically directed at children.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Security

Since HiTech does not store user personal data, no user database or account-based personal information is maintained through the experience.

However, we still aim to use reasonable technical and organizational measures appropriate to the nature of the product and its functionality.

10. Your Privacy Choices

Because HiTech does not create user accounts or store personal data, there is generally no account data to access, correct, or delete.

  • Users may stop using the experience at any time.
  • Users may also control device-level permissions, such as camera access, through the relevant browser or device settings.

Please note that camera access is necessary for the real-time AR mirror experience to function.

11. Geographic Scope

HiTech is currently intended for use in Egypt and is available as a web-based experience.

12. Third-Party Services

HiTech uses a Camera Kit provider to support AR features and real-time effect rendering.

We recommend that users also review the privacy information of the browser, device, and any third-party technology involved in delivering the web experience, where applicable.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the product, legal requirements, or operational needs.

When we make updates, we will revise the “Last Updated” date at the top of this page.
